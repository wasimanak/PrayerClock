Introduction

Welcome to PrayerClock. This Privacy Policy explains how Alpha4 Technologies ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. PrayerClock is designed to provide accurate Islamic prayer times, Azan alerts, Qibla direction, and Tasbih counter — all with minimal data collection.

📍

Information We Collect

PrayerClock collects only the minimum information required to function:

  • Location Data — Used to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. It is also sent to a third-party weather service (Open-Meteo) to provide current temperature. We do not store your location on our own servers.
  • Device Identifiers — We collect basic identifiers like ANDROID_ID and device model to monitor app usage, provide updates, and ensure the stability of our services via Firebase.
🔐

App Permissions Explained

Permission Why We Need It
ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION
ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION		 To calculate prayer times, Qibla direction, and fetch local weather data.
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM
USE_EXACT_ALARM		 To deliver Azan alerts precisely at prayer times.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE
FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK		 To play the Azan audio reliably in the background.

⚙️

How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate accurate Salah (prayer) times.
  • Provide real-time weather/temperature updates.
  • Determine Qibla direction.
  • Deliver timely Azan and Jamat notifications.
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs through anonymous analytics.
🔗

Third-Party Services

PrayerClock uses the following third-party services:

  • Open-Meteo — Used to fetch weather data based on your location. Open-Meteo Privacy →
  • Google AdMob — Displays ads. Google Privacy Policy →
  • Firebase (Database, Analytics, Crashlytics) — Used for tracking app versions, stability, and anonymous usage stats.
🚫

Data Sharing & Sale

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties outside of the services listed above.

Your location data is used exclusively on-device to compute prayer times and is never transmitted to our servers or any external service.

🗄️

Data Retention

PrayerClock does not maintain any user accounts or server-side databases. All preferences and settings are stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Anonymous analytics and crash data retained by Firebase follow Google's data retention policies.

👶

Children's Privacy

PrayerClock is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

🛡️

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Since PrayerClock processes location data exclusively on-device and does not transmit it to external servers, the risk of data interception is minimal.

Changes to This Policy

Continued use of PrayerClock after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
